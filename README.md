Ascend Bit Company Limited (ABC) is a Bangkok-based **Web 3.0 technology venture**, founded in 2021 by seasoned digital venture builders and technology leaders.

ABC's core focus is developing loyalty Web 3.0 solutions that enable brands and partners to revolutionize their marketing, loyalty, and Customer Relationship Management (CRM) activities. As an affiliate of the Charoen Pokphand (C.P.) Group, one of Southeast Asia's largest conglomerates, ABC leverages proprietary access and industry insight across a vast ecosystem of retail and consumer brands.

Our solutions deliver key benefits including real-time transaction settlement and reconciliation, differentiated consumer engagement, novel brand insights, and increased revenue generation opportunities.

The ABC Internship Program is designed to provide hands-on, high-impact experience within a lean-startup enterprise environment, focusing on cutting-edge Web 3.0 and cloud-native technologies.

Internships at ABC are designed to immerse trainees in critical functional areas, providing skills and experience in:

| Track | Key Objectives |

### Key Projects and Assignments

Interns contribute directly to strategic company initiatives through challenging R&D and implementation projects.
